# USAGE Guide for Teachers and Students

## For Teachers

### Getting Started
1. Log in with your teacher credentials.
2. Access the dashboard to view class schedules and assignments.

### Creating Assignments
- Navigate to the Assignments section.
- Click 'Create Assignment'.
- Fill out the necessary fields and submit.

### Monitoring Student Progress
- Go to the Student Performance tab to view analytics on student submissions and grades.

## For Students

### Logging In
1. Use your student credentials to log in.
2. You will be directed to your personal dashboard.

### Accessing Assignments
- Find assignments listed on your dashboard.
- Click on each assignment for details and submission instructions.

### Submitting Work
- Use the submission feature provided for each assignment to upload your files.
- Ensure to follow the formatting guidelines provided.

### Checking Grades
- After submission, navigate to the Grades section to view your scores and feedback.
